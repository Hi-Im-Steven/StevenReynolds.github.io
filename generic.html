About me

Father - Husband - Traveler - veteran

Who am I?

Hello, my name is Steven Reynolds; I am a 30-year-old husband and father of an amazing 2-year-old!

I joined the United States Marine Corps in 2013 and worked on the RQ-7B and RQ-21A model Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAV). I deployed on the 24th MEU aboard USS Mesa Verde in 2017 and again on the 13th MEU aboard USS John P Murtha in 2019. I have been lucky enough to see a lot of the world, including countries such as:
Spain - Greece - Crete - Qatar - Saudia Arabia - Philippines - Okinawa - Guam - Israel - The Old City of Jerusalem

In 2020 my wife and I got news that our first born was on the way! I quit deploying and started going to school where I ultimately got my AAS in Management Information Systems from Columbus State Community College. When im not working I'm hanging out with my wife and kid, traveling when we can and enjoying our adventure as a family. We love to go camping in Hocking Hills and also try to vacation to Hilton Head Island every year.

Why Data Analytics?

Data, to me, is like an intriguing puzzle awaiting assembly. I enjoy the challenge of piecing together scattered fragments to unveil the bigger picture. By delving into diverse sectors, including Airbnb, insurance, and finance, I continuously explore the potential of data, revealing actionable insights that inform strategic decision-making and drive business transformations.

This portfolio website serves as a gateway into my world of data analysis and visualization. Here, I showcase projects that demonstrate my technical expertise and my ability to transform raw data into compelling narratives. Through my work, I aim to captivate potential employers and convey my relentless curiosity for unlocking untapped data potential.

Join me on an exciting journey of exploration, analysis, and visualization. Together, let's unleash the power of data and embrace its infinite possibilities.
